> **Mission:** Create a measurable and transparent model that tracks, verifies, and rewards eco-friendly activities performed by women, converting everyday actions into verified carbon reduction data.

## ⚠️ Problem Statement

Women in rural India perform over 70% of eco-practices, including composting, organic farming, waste segregation, and water conservation. They are the backbone of grassroots environmental stewardship. 

Despite their critical role, women's contributions go unrecognized in climate finance. No mechanism exists to quantify their impact or connect them to carbon credit markets. Zero structured reward systems exist for community-level carbon reduction, leaving rural women entirely excluded from the $114B global carbon credit market.

### The Three Critical Gaps
* **No Quantification:** No proper mechanism to measure women's carbon reduction impact.
* **No Reward System:** No structured incentives for sustainable practices at the grassroots level.
* **Low Awareness:** Minimal understanding of carbon credits and sustainability economics.

---

## 💡 Core Value Proposition

* 📱 **Simple Digital Interface:** Mobile and web app for easy eco-action logging.
* 🤝 **SHG Network:** Leverages existing Self-Help Group infrastructure for trust.
* ✅ **Verified Impact:** Photo, GPS, and community verification for credibility.
* 💰 **Direct Rewards:** Carbon credits convert to cash or voucher incentives.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1.  **Eco Action:** Women perform sustainable practices like composting, organic farming, waste segregation, and tree planting.
2.  **Log & Capture:** Record via the app with photo proof, GPS location, date stamp, and action type.
3.  **Verify:** SHG coordinators review and approve submissions with community validation.
4.  **Calculate:** The system converts actions to CO₂e reduction using standardized carbon accounting.
5.  **Reward:** Verified credits convert to monetary incentives.

---

## ✨ Features of the App

* **Multilingual Support:** Accessed primarily in English, with active support for Hindi and Kannada.
* **Dual Authorization:** Register and login as either a User or a Verifier.
* **User Dashboard:** Log eco-friendly actions using a GPS-enabled camera for verification, check wallet balances for earned credits, view community updates, and access training resources to increase awareness.
* **Verifier Dashboard:** View actions logged by users to either approve or reject them. Users receive rewards based on approval and the calculated carbon reduction weight of their specific action.
